Tribals.io

Immersive 3D Survival Game

About Tribals.io

Tribals.io is an immersive 3D multiplayer survival game created by OnRush Studio, blending mechanics from popular survival games like Rust, Minecraft, and ARK Survival. The game takes place on a dynamic and dangerous island where players must gather resources, build shelters, and protect themselves from environmental hazards and other players.

Core Gameplay

  • Resource Gathering

    Players begin by scavenging for basic resources like wood, stone, and food. Resources are gathered through exploration or by defeating enemies, including animals and other players.

  • Crafting and Survival

    Materials are used to craft tools, weapons, and structures, enabling players to upgrade their equipment and fortify their bases. Survival mechanics like managing hunger and thirst are crucial.

  • Threats and Challenges

    The island is filled with threats such as hostile animals and rival tribes, making strategic decision-making crucial for longevity.

Multiplayer and Social Dynamics

The multiplayer aspect allows players to team up and form clans for cooperative survival or competitive raids. Clan members can coordinate to defend their shared base, attack rival groups, or engage in large-scale battles.

Customization and Accessibility

Tribals.io offers a high degree of customization, and the game is designed for accessibility, supporting various input methods and a streamlined interface.

Free-to-Play and Community

The game is free-to-play, and its community-driven development approach includes suggestions from players, fostering a loyal fanbase.
